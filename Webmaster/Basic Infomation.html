ABOUT US

Welcome to Space Horizon, the premier provider of space tourism experiences. We are a team of dedicated professionals with a passion for space exploration and a commitment to making the dream of space travel a reality for people from all walks of life. Our company was founded with the goal of providing safe and affordable access to space for everyone. We believe that the future of space travel is not just for astronauts and scientists, but for anyone who has ever looked up at the night sky and dreamed of experiencing the wonder of space firsthand.

Subtitle 1

We offer a wide range of space tourism experiences, from suborbital flights to orbiting vacations, as well as custom-designed trips for groups and individuals. All of our trips are fully licensed and insured, and our pilots and crew are highly trained and experienced.

Subtitle 2

We are constantly working to improve and expand our offerings, and we are committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience. From the moment you book your trip with us, we will be with you every step of the way, providing support and assistance to ensure that your space travel experience is truly unforgettable.
